# Justice League Academy

**Superhero Training for Everyone.**

Justice League Academy (JLA) is a free, open learning platform where anyone can train their mind, body, spirit and technical skills — and earn karma for every step of the journey. It is one of the core applications within the [OASIS Omniverse](https://oasisomniverse.one) ecosystem, a Web4 platform built around universal avatars, karma and a mission to unlock human potential.

**No one is turned away.** JLA has a standing second-chance policy: people experiencing homelessness, special needs, or difficult circumstances are warmly welcomed and supported.

---

## What is JLA?

JLA allows anyone to:

- **Enrol in courses** — across 6 disciplines: Mind, Body, Conscious Coding, Service, Arts and Science
- **Earn karma for learning** — every course completed adds karma to your OASIS universal avatar
- **Teach a class** — share your knowledge and earn +750 karma per class submitted
- **Unlock jobs at OASIS** — high karma earners are invited to apply for paid roles across the ecosystem
- **Access founding documents** — the original 2013 Training and Junior Programme PDFs that JLA was built on

## Running Locally

No build step required. Open `index.html` directly in a browser, or serve with any static server:

```bash
npx serve .
# or
python -m http.server 8080
```
