OUR VISION

Technology in Service of Love

"As one wise person once said — 'I have a dream'… well, we have a dream too."

A vision where we can all co-exist in peace and harmony — not only with ourselves but with the planet. A vision where war, fear and separation are things of the past. A vision where love is the predominant force on Earth.

We want to reach this vision by using technology for good rather than evil — to connect people rather than disconnect them, to improve the quality of life for every soul on the planet, and to restore our world to her former beauty.

THE CONSCIOUS CODER SERIES

Holistic Skills for Holistic Coders

Our unique *4Coders modules prove what we've always known: happier, healthier, more balanced people make better developers. These are built into your training day — not extras you have to find time for.

🧘
YOGA4CODERS

Yoga dramatically improves focus, attention and energy levels. This module dives deep into how yoga benefits not just your physical and mental health — but directly improves your coding. Most people are not aware of how many benefits yoga gives you.

🧠
MEDITATION4CODERS

Meditation improves mental clarity, emotional resilience and creative problem-solving — all critical coding skills. Learn how a consistent practice makes you not only a better developer but a calmer, happier human being.

🌿
MINDFULNESS4CODERS

Present-moment awareness transforms how you write code — fewer bugs, better focus, deeper flow states. Mindfulness is a superpower for any developer. This module shows you how to build it into your working day.

🥗
NUTRITION4CODERS

What you eat directly affects cognitive function, energy levels and mood. Learn how the right nutrition supercharges your brain and your output. Your code is only as good as the fuel behind it.

🌍
HOLISTICLIFESTYLE4CODERS

The full picture — combining yoga, meditation, mindfulness, nutrition and being in nature into a holistic daily practice that makes you effective, energised and at peace. The Conscious Programming Methodology in full.

💃
DANCE4CODERS

Dance is one of the most powerful healing modalities on earth. It releases energy blocks, boosts creativity, elevates mood and reconnects you to joy. A joyful coder is a creative coder. Come dance your way to better code.

THE JLA WAY

A Different Kind of Academy

We don't just teach differently — we operate differently. These are the principles that make JLA unlike anything else out there.

🎮
FLEXI PROJECTS

Work on what you actually want to work on. If a project doesn't excite you, switch to one that does. Fresh eyes on a problem are always welcome here — and curiosity is never wasted.

💡
CREATE YOUR OWN PROJECTS

Got an idea for something world-changing? If it fits our vision of helping people, you can help make it a reality. You'll be fully credited and earn karma — plus the chance to see your idea actually ship.

🌅
FRIDAY AFTERNOON PROJECTS

Every Friday afternoon is yours. Work on your own ideas, build a prototype, explore something that sparks your imagination. If it has potential, we'll reward you and help you make it real.

🏆
REWARDS & INCENTIVES

Beyond karma, outstanding work earns real rewards — vouchers, experiences, and for the truly exceptional: luxury holidays and cash prizes. Come up with a brilliant idea and get credited and rewarded for it.

🤝
CO-CREATORS, NOT STUDENTS

We operate a flat structure — no hierarchy, no performance reviews, no stressful deadlines. Every voice is heard, everyone is treated as an equal. You're not a resource here. You're a co-creator of our new world.

🧠
STATE-OF-THE-ART TEACHING

We use real-time performance metrics to adjust content and techniques to your learning style. Every session is optimised to help you become the best you possibly can be — not just another student in the crowd.
